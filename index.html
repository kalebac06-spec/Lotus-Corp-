About Lotus Corp

Founded in China, Lotus Corp has grown into a multi-national financial enterprise, renowned for combining innovative investment strategies with long-term urban development initiatives. Our vision has always been to not just generate profits, but also to contribute meaningfully to the communities in which we operate.

Our recent expansion into Japan, starting in Shibuya, marks a new chapter. The Shibuya Revival Project is our flagship initiative, designed to rejuvenate urban spaces while offering opportunities for local businesses, investors, and residents alike. We focus on mall expansions, upgraded residential living, and city-wide improvements that anticipate future challenges.

From planning sustainable urban growth to preparing for natural events like seasonal storms, Lotus Corp is committed to building resilience into the cities we touch. Our approach blends finance, innovation, and community well-being, ensuring growth that benefits both stakeholders and citizens.
